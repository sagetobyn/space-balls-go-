Space Balls GO!

This privacy policy describes how Hassium Labs ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in the Space Balls GO! mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Space Balls GO! is a single-player offline game that:

  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not collect names, emails, or any personal data
  • Stores game progress locally on your device only

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect certain information for advertising purposes. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Google Play Games Services

If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games, Google manages that data according to their privacy policy.

Data Storage

Game progress (level completion, stars earned, settings) is stored locally on your device using standard app storage. This data is not transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

Our game does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The game is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
