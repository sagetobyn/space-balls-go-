# Screenshot Guide for Space Balls GO

## Phone Screenshots (9:16 aspect ratio, min 1080px wide)

### Required Screenshots (minimum 4):

1. **Main Menu** 
   - Shows the beautiful space-themed menu with PLAY button
   - Capture from main menu screen

2. **Early Gameplay (Level 2-5)**
   - Clean level with simple obstacles
   - Shows the cyan player ball clearly
   - Shows exit portal and star collectibles

3. **Mid-Game Action (Level 15-20)**
   - Orbiting obstacles in action
   - Dynamic gameplay moment
   - Stars being collected

4. **Advanced Level (Level 30-40)**
   - Complex obstacle patterns
   - Shows game depth and variety

### Optional Screenshots (recommended for better store presence):

5. **Level Selection Screen**
   - Shows progression with locked/unlocked levels
   - Star ratings visible

6. **Level Complete Screen**
   - Shows victory with 3 stars earned
   - "LEVEL COMPLETE" text visible

7. **Chasing Enemy Gameplay**
   - Blue chasing balls in action
   - Adds excitement to listing

8. **Rotating/Pulsing Obstacles**
   - Shows variety of obstacle types

## Tablet Screenshots

Use the same scenes but capture on tablet for proper aspect ratio.

## How to Capture:

### On Android Device:
1. Press Power + Volume Down simultaneously
2. Screenshot saved to Gallery

### On Emulator:
1. Click camera icon in emulator toolbar
2. Or use Android Studio's screenshot feature

## Tips for Great Screenshots:

- Capture during exciting moments (near miss with obstacle)
- Ensure player ball is clearly visible
- Show variety of obstacle types
- Keep UI elements visible
- Use highest resolution possible
- Avoid any debug text or FPS counters

## To Unlock All Levels for Screenshots:

In GameScreen.tsx, you can temporarily modify the level unlock logic,
or use the game's progress system to unlock levels for screenshot purposes.
